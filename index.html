# About

The HyperMedia Research Group (HMRG) was founded at Montana State University to provide support and infrastructure for research on hypermedia & hypermedia-adjacent technologies.

# Projects

HMRG affiliated research projects include:

  • htmx - A generalization of the hypermedia control mechanic
  • Triptych - Moving concepts of generalized hypermedia controls into the HTML specification
  • fixi.js - A minimalist generalization of the hypermedia control mechanic
  • hyperscript - An experimental, hypertext-friendly scripting language
  • Hyperview - A mobile hypermedia (HXML) & hypermedia client
